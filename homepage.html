What do Christians believe?

Trinity God

We believe in the God Trinity who comes in 3 persons but 1 essential, who is also Alpha and Omega, The Omnipresent.

Salvation inside the Church

We believe in the Salvation that only come from the church and outside it no salvation.

Saints

We believe in the Saints, Holy men of God, who before them and God the Father Jesus is to show his majesty power.
see 1 Thessalonians 3: 13.

Resurrection of Body

We believe in the Resurrection of Body that is given promises by Lord Jesus after believing in him, only Savior. that is the second eternal life.

Theology & Doctrines

We believe in every legal doctrines that is firstly come from the council of nicea. And this is continouing to this day.

Judgment Day

We believe in Judgment day which is the judgment to every nations that is to done by Jesus. this is where you will be saved and get no judgement upon you after believe in Jesus and do the commandments of God.

Hades & Shamayim

We believe in the hades that God made it to the devil and the rest of man to the judgment day. hades God made to the devil means hell and the rest of man means grave.

Communion of the Saints

We believe not only in the Saints but also their communion because God is in their communion and it is very sacred.

Archangel

We believe in the Archangel of God because they are also Saints. example St.Michael Archangel the goodwar man of God and St.Gabriel Archangel the messanger of Gospel.
